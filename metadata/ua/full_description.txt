A native <a href='https://diasporafoundation.org'>diaspora*</a> client built with Flutter for iOS and Android.

insporation* is designed for everyday use on diaspora* pods, including reading your stream, following conversations, and publishing posts from your phone.

diaspora* is a decentralized social network: people sign up on independently run servers (pods) and can still connect across the wider network. Its core principles are decentralization, freedom, and privacy.

Compatible with diaspora* 0.9 and later.
